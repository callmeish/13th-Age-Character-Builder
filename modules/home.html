How to Use this Site

It's easy!

  1. Click on "Character Creation" on the left for a brief rundown of what's involved in making a character in 13th Age.
  2. Move to "Races" when you're ready to begin.
  3. Work your way through the individual steps of character creation. Each step should each take you to the next upon completion.
  4. Copy your completed character's stats to your character sheet.
